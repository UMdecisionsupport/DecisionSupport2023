### Logic Notation

- disjunction: ∨
- conjunction: ∧
- literal: B
- entails: ⊨
- negation: ¬
- clauses: (A∧B) ∨ (B∧¬C)
- implication: ⇒
- equivalence: ⇔, ≡
- derives: ⊢
