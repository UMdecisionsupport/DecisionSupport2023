## Solutions to the Medium Exercises

### CNF I
(p ∨ q) ∧ (p ∧ r) is already in CNF. This can be more easily seen when the second pair of brackets are dropped and it is written as (p ∨ q) ∧ p ∧ r.

### CNF II
- (A∨B∨C)∧(¬A∨¬C)∧(¬B∨¬C)
- (p ∨ r) ∧ (¬q ∨ r)
- (¬p ∨ q) ∧ (¬q ∨ p)

### DPLL Notation
(A∨C)∧(¬A∨¬B∨C)∧(A∨¬C)
